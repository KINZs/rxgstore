<p>When using this item you are presented with a list of songs that are currently loaded by the server. </p>
<p>The server chooses a selection of songs randomly whenever it restarts. </p>

<p>When you select a song, this item will be removed from your inventory and placed in front of you featuring the song you selected.</p>
